My friend René drove his Citroën to a café in Zürich. "I'd like the crème brûlée," he told the maître d'. The naïve résumé of the day's spécialités: piña colada, jalapeño poppers, and a château wine from the Côte d'Azur.

"That'll be €45," she said — not $45, mind you. "We also have børnemenuen for the kids: smørrebrød and crêpes." François smiled and whispered, "C'est la vie — même les prix sont élégants ici."
